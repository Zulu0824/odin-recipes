Grilled Cheese

Home

Description

Grilled cheese is a classic comfort food. It is one of the easiest and most consumed foods in the world


Today we are going to prepare a Grilled Cheese using the following ingredients and instructions

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Block of parmesan cheese
  • Cheese slices
  • Garlic butter or Salted butter

Steps

  1. Heat up a frying pan and add butter of your choice.
  2. Toast the bread slices on the pan (both sides)
  3. Add your selection of cheese to the bread and layer the bread on the cheese.
  4. Add more butter to taste.
  5. Remove the bread from the pan and enjoy your delicious Grilled Cheese.
